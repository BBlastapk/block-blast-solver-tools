# Android Performance Optimization

Block Blast performance depends heavily on device memory management and background process stability.

This guide covers practical Android optimizations for smoother gameplay sessions.

Official project resource:
https://blockblastdownload.com/

---

## Recommended Device Requirements

Minimum:
- Android 5.0+
- 3 GB RAM
- Quad-core processor

Recommended:
- Android 11+
- 6 GB RAM
- Stable storage space above 2 GB free

---

## Reduce Lag During Gameplay

### Close Background Apps

Applications like:
- Chrome
- TikTok
- YouTube
- Facebook

consume RAM continuously and may reduce gameplay stability.

Before launching Block Blast:
- clear recent apps
- restart the device if necessary

---

## Disable Battery Restrictions

Some Android systems aggressively pause background processes.

Path may vary by device:

Settings → Apps → Block Blast → Battery → Unrestricted

This improves:
- revive consistency
- smoother transitions
- reduced freezing

---

## Keep Device Storage Clean

Low storage creates:
- slower loading
- delayed rendering
- installation issues

Maintain:
- at least 2 GB free space
- regular cache cleanup

---

## Improve Thermal Stability

Long sessions may increase device temperature.

To reduce overheating:
- lower screen brightness
- avoid charging while playing
- disable unnecessary overlays

---

## Offline Gameplay Benefits

Offline sessions often provide:
- lower battery usage
- fewer interruptions
- improved stability

Airplane mode may help reduce background network activity during gameplay.
