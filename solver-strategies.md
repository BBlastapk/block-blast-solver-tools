# Solver Strategies

Advanced Block Blast gameplay depends heavily on spatial prediction and board preservation.

## High-Level Placement Rules

- Preserve center flexibility
- Keep recovery lanes available
- Avoid isolated dead zones
- Prioritize future placements over immediate clears

## Efficient Combo Building

Combo chains become easier when:
- lower rows remain partially open
- edge congestion stays controlled
- long pieces are reserved strategically

## Risk Management

Bad placements usually happen after:
- panic clears
- forced corner stacking
- early revive dependence

Consistent players focus on maintaining future placement options instead of maximizing short-term points.
